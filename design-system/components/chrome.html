An italic Cormorant lede sits beneath every section head — short, declarative, never more than two lines.

How fit do I need to be?
Comfortable walking 6–8 hours at altitude. Three months of training recommended. A medical fitness certificate is mandatory.
What about altitude sickness?
Real, manageable, and planned for. Two acclimatisation days, oxygen on every vehicle, doctor on the team.

A warm welcome

Whoever you are, wherever you come from, you are welcome here.

You may have arrived after a long search, or you may have stumbled upon us by chance. Either way — there is a seat for you at this table. No question is too small. No past is disqualifying. No background matters. This is a space where you are free to be exactly, entirely, beautifully yourself.

Our purpose

From humankind,
to kind humans.

Mohanji Foundation exists to raise awareness from selfishness to selflessness — to awaken kindness as a way of life. We believe humanity is the best religion, and the highest practice is ahimsa.

Kindness as a way of life

Not a feeling, but a practice. We train ourselves to meet every moment, every being, with compassion — beginning with ourselves.

Ahimsa & conscious living

Non-violence in thought, word and action — including our food, our choices, and how we walk upon the earth. A plant-based, cruelty-free life is a natural expression of this.

Service without boundary

Food for all. Disaster relief. Dignity to the poor. We serve beyond nationality, race, religion or species — because there are no others.

Free will, always

No doctrine. No prescribed path. We offer what we have learned, and you take only what resonates. Your journey is yours to walk.

Brahmarishi Mohanji
For you, the one still searching

A humanitarian,
a presence.

Mohanji is a global humanitarian whose life's work is to awaken kindness in the human heart. His mission is to raise awareness from selfishness to selflessness, and to remind each person of their own inherent sovereignty.

He stands in the timeless lineage of the Himalayan masters, yet prescribes no doctrine. His teaching has only one instruction: understand, accept and express your uniqueness in the world. Through Mohanji Foundation, he has established a global family active in over ninety countries — a home for seekers of every path.

The highest path is the one that returns you to yourself.
